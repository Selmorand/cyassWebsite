Landlord Workflow

Track property condition and manage maintenance with evidence.

Landlords inspect properties to track condition between tenants, prove damage, and manage maintenance. CYA Pro also lets you create contractor jobs and invite tradespeople directly from the app.

1 Sign Up

Create your account and select “Landlord”. You get 1 free inspection report.

2 Add Your Property

Register each property you manage with its full address. GPS coordinates are captured for verification.

3 Create an Inspection Report

Start a new report for any property. Add rooms with predefined checklists for each room type.

4 Inspect Room by Room

Rate every item’s condition, take photos, add notes. Document any existing damage or wear.

5 Record Video Walkthroughs

Optional video walkthroughs for each room to capture the full picture.

6 Review & Finalize

Review the colour-coded summary, finalize to lock the report with a timestamp.

7 Generate & Share PDF

Professional PDF with all evidence. Share with tenants or keep for your records.
