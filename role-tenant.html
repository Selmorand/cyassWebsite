Tenant Workflow

Protect your deposit with a professional condition report

Tenants inspect the property at move-in and move-out to protect their deposit and avoid disputes. CYA Pro guides you through every step — from signing up to generating a court-ready PDF report.

1 Sign Up

Create your account with your name, email, and select "Tenant" as your role. Google sign-in is also available. You get 1 free report.

2 Add Your Property

Enter your rental property address and details. Your device's GPS coordinates are captured automatically to verify the location.

3 Create a Report

Select your property and start a new inspection report. Add rooms (bedrooms, bathrooms, kitchen, etc.) — each has a predefined checklist.

4 Inspect Room by Room

For each room, rate the condition of every item (walls, floors, windows, etc.) as Good, Fair, Poor, or Urgent Repair. Take photos and add notes for anything that isn't perfect.

5 Record Video Walkthroughs

Optionally record a video walkthrough of each room for extra evidence. Max 2 minutes per room.

6 Review Your Report

See a colour-coded summary of every room and item. Check that everything is documented before finalizing.

7 Finalize & Lock

Lock the report to make it immutable. A timestamp is set — this is what makes it court-ready evidence.

8 Generate & Share PDF

A professional A4 PDF is generated with all your photos, ratings, notes, and property details. Download it or share via a public link — no login needed to view.
