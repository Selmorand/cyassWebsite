How It Works

From signup to professional PDF report in minutes.

1

Sign Up

Create your account with your name, email, and role. Choose from Tenant, Landlord, Buyer, Seller, Agent, or Contractor. Google sign-in is also available. Agents and contractors get 2 free reports; everyone else gets 1.

2

Add a Property

Enter the property name, type (house, flat, townhouse, etc.), and full South African address. Your device's GPS coordinates are captured automatically to verify the location.

3

Create a Report

Select your property and add rooms. Each room type (bedroom, bathroom, kitchen, exterior, etc.) has its own predefined inspection checklist. Agents can choose which role's template to use.

4

Inspect Room by Room

For each room, optionally record a video walkthrough. Then rate each item's condition (Good, Fair, Poor, Urgent Repair, or N/A), add notes for any issues, and take photos. Everything auto-saves as you go.

5

Review & Complete

The Report Summary shows your property details, room-by-room condition breakdown (colour-coded), photo thumbnails, and overall statistics. Review everything, then mark as complete.

6

Finalize

Lock the report to make it immutable. A finalized timestamp is set, and the report can no longer be edited — this is what makes it court-ready.

7

Generate & Share Your PDF

A professional A4 PDF is generated with branding, property details, condition overview, room-by-room detail pages with photos, and a legal disclaimer. Download it or share via a public link — no login needed to view.
