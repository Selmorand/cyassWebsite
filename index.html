Why Cover Your Assets?

Paper checklists don't cut it anymore. Protect yourself with professional digital documentation.

The Problem

  • Paper checklists get lost or damaged
  • No timestamps or proof of when inspection happened
  • No photo evidence attached to specific items
  • Won't hold up in disputes or legal proceedings
  • No way to share reports digitally

The Cover Your Assets Solution

  • Digital reports stored securely in the cloud
  • GPS-verified, timestamped inspections
  • Photos and video evidence per item
  • Professional court-ready PDF reports
  • Shareable public links — no login needed to view
