Buyer Workflow

Pre-purchase due diligence — document defects before signing

Buyers inspect a property before transfer to identify defects, risks, and compliance issues. CYA Pro helps you document everything before you commit.

1 Sign Up

Create your account and select "Buyer". You get 1 free report.

2 Add the Property

Enter the property address you're considering buying. GPS coordinates verify the location.

3 Create an Inspection Report

Start a comprehensive inspection. Buyer templates include structural indicators, compliance documents, certificates, and more.

4 Inspect Room by Room

Rate the condition of walls, floors, ceilings, plumbing, electrical, and more. Take photos of every defect.

5 Check Compliance Documents

Document whether electrical COC, gas COC, plumbing COC, beetle certificate, building plans, and occupation certificate are present and valid.

6 Review Structural Indicators

Check for foundation cracks, roof sagging, wall cracks, and settlement signs. Rate severity and photograph evidence.

7 Record Video Walkthroughs

Optional video walkthroughs for each room to capture the full picture.

8 Finalize & Generate PDF

Lock the report and generate a professional PDF. Share it with your conveyancer, attorney, or keep it as pre-purchase evidence.
