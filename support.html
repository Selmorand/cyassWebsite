Is my first report really free?

Yes. Every new user gets at least 1 free report (agents and contractors get 2). No credit card required to sign up.

Does it work offline?

Yes. CYA Pro works offline and syncs your data automatically when you're back online. Perfect for areas with poor connectivity.

Can I share reports with others?

Yes. Every finalized report gets a shareable public link. Anyone can view it without needing a CYA Pro account.

What payment methods are accepted?

We use Yoco for secure payments. Credit cards, debit cards, Google Pay, and Apple Pay are all supported.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes. Agent Pro subscriptions can be cancelled at any time. Your access continues until the end of your current billing period.
