PART 3: CONTRACTOR JOBS SYSTEM (NEW)
================================================================================

In addition to the inspection template above, contractors have a dedicated
job management system for maintenance/repair work.

CONTRACTOR PROFILE
------------------
Contractors complete a 4-step profile after accepting their first job invite:

Step 1 - Business & Trade:
  - Business Name              [text] REQUIRED
  - Mobile Number              [text] REQUIRED
  - Primary Trade              [dropdown: 14 trade types] REQUIRED
  - Secondary Trades           [multi-select toggle buttons]
  - Province                   [dropdown: 9 SA provinces] REQUIRED
  - Town/City                  [text] REQUIRED
  - Service Radius (km)        [number: 1-500]

Step 2 - Registrations & Accreditations:
  - Multiple registrations, each with:
    - Registration Body        [dropdown: ECA/ECB/SAQCC Gas/PIRB/ECSA/SACAP/NHBRC/FPASA/Other]
    - Registration Number      [text]
    - Expiry Date              [date] (optional)

Step 3 - Capabilities:
  Certificates they can issue:
    - Electrical CoC            [checkbox]
    - Gas CoC                   [checkbox]
    - Plumbing/Water CoC        [checkbox]
    - Electric Fence CoC        [checkbox]
    - Beetle/Pest Certificate   [checkbox]
  Documents they can provide:
    - Repair Quotations         [checkbox]
    - Warranties & Guarantees   [checkbox]
    - Completion Certificates   [checkbox]

Step 4 - Operational:
  - Emergency Callout           [checkbox]
  - Base Callout Fee (R)        [number] (optional)
  - Additional Notes            [textarea]

JOB MANAGEMENT
--------------
14 Trade Types:
  electrician, gas_installer, plumber, builder, painter, tiler, roofer,
  carpenter, locksmith, pest_control, waterproofer, glazier,
  general_maintenance, other

Each trade has 5-10 specific job types. Example (electrician):
  fix_lights, install_geyser, fault_finding, rewire, db_board,
  issue_electrical_coc, other

Job Creation Inputs:
  - Property                   [select from existing properties] REQUIRED
  - Trade Type                 [dropdown: 14 types] REQUIRED
  - Job Type                   [dropdown: filtered by trade] REQUIRED
  - Custom Title               [text] (only if jobType = 'other')
  - Area of Property           [dropdown: 13 areas] REQUIRED
  - Urgency                    [buttons: low/medium/high/critical] REQUIRED
  - Description/Notes          [textarea] REQUIRED

13 Areas of Property:
  kitchen, bathroom, bedroom, living_room, garage, garden, roof,
  exterior, pool, fence, driveway, storeroom, other

Job Status Flow:
  not_started → in_progress → completed
       │              │
       │              ├── awaiting_parts → in_progress
       │              │
       │              └── awaiting_approval → completed
       │
       └── cancelled

Job Execution Inputs:
  - Before Photos              [photo-multi] REQUIRED for completion
  - During Photos              [photo-multi] (optional)
  - After Photos               [photo-multi] REQUIRED for completion
  - Certificates               [document upload] REQUIRED for 5 job types
  - Description/Notes          [textarea] (auto-saves)

5 Certificate-Required Job Types:
  - issue_electrical_coc       (Electrical Certificate of Compliance)
  - issue_gas_coc              (Gas Certificate of Compliance)
  - issue_plumbing_coc         (Plumbing/Water Certificate of Compliance)
  - issue_electric_fence_coc   (Electric Fence Certificate of Compliance)
  - issue_beetle_cert          (Beetle/Pest Certificate)

JOB INVITE FLOW (PUBLIC)
-------------------------
1. Owner/agent creates job → gets invite link
2. Shares via WhatsApp, email, or copy link
3. Contractor clicks link → public 3-step flow:
   Step 1: Confirm identity (email or phone)
   Step 2: Set password + name → auto-signup as contractor
   Step 3: Job assigned → redirect to profile (if incomplete) or job detail

Invite link format: https://app.cyass.co.za/job-invite/{jobId}

Share message includes: job title, property, trade type, reference ID
