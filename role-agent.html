Agent Workflow

Run your inspection business from your phone

Built for Real Estate Professionals

Agents conduct inspections to support transactions and provide professional reports. With Agent Pro, you get unlimited reports, team management, any inspection template, and contractor job management.

1 Sign Up

Create your account and select "Agent". You get 2 free reports to try the app. Optionally provide your agency name.

2 Add Properties

Register any property for inspection. Manage multiple properties from your dashboard.

3 Choose Any Template

Agents can use any role's inspection template — tenant, landlord, buyer, seller, or contractor perspective. One tool for every scenario.

4 Inspect Room by Room

Rate conditions, take photos, add notes. Predefined checklists for every room type.

5 Record Video Walkthroughs

Optional video per room for comprehensive documentation.

6 Generate Professional PDFs

Branded A4 reports with your agency name, all photos, ratings, and a legal disclaimer. Share via public link.

7 Manage Your Team

Invite up to 3 team members to share your Agent Pro subscription. They get unlimited reports at no extra cost.

8 Create Contractor Jobs

Create maintenance or repair jobs from 14 trade types. Set urgency, describe the work, and select the area of the property.

9 Invite Contractors

Share job invite links via WhatsApp, email, or copy link. Contractors sign up in 3 steps — no app download needed.

10 Track Job Progress

Monitor all jobs from your dashboard. View statuses, photos, and compliance certificates.

11 Subscribe to Agent Pro

R499/month for unlimited reports, team management, any template, and contractor job management.
