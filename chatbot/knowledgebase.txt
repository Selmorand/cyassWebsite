1. ABOUT CYA PRO
-----------------

CYA Pro is a property condition inspection app built for the South African market. It replaces paper checklists used during move-in/move-out inspections, property sales, and maintenance assessments with a professional digital workflow.

Business name: C Y Assets (trading as Cover Your Assets / CYass)
App name: CYA Pro (also referred to as CYA Pro or C-Y-ASSETS)

The app digitises the entire property inspection process with GPS verification, timestamps, photo and video evidence, and professional PDF report generation. It is specifically designed for South Africa with SA address formats, SA provinces, Rand pricing, and local payment processing.


2. HOW IT WORKS
---------------

The full workflow has 7 steps:

Step 1 -- Sign Up
Create an account with your full name, email, password, and role. Choose from Tenant, Landlord, Buyer, Seller, Agent, or Contractor. Google sign-in is also available. Email verification is required before first login. Agents and contractors get 2 free reports. Everyone else gets 1 free report.

Step 2 -- Add a Property
Enter the property name (e.g. "23 Oak Lane"), property type (House, Townhouse, Flat, Cluster, Cottage, Granny Flat, or Other), and optionally a unit number, complex name, or estate name. Enter the full South African address: street number, street name, suburb, city, province (from all 9 SA provinces), and postal code. GPS coordinates are captured automatically from your device to verify the location. On mobile, device GPS is used (warns if accuracy exceeds 200m). On desktop, network location is used (less accurate, with a "Skip GPS" option).

Step 3 -- Create a Report
Select a property from your list and add rooms to the report. Each room has a name (e.g. "Main Bedroom") and a type. There are 7 room types: Standard, Bathroom, Kitchen, Patio, Outbuilding, Exterior, and Special Features. Each type has its own predefined inspection categories. Agents can choose which role's inspection template to use (tenant, landlord, buyer, seller, or contractor perspective).

Step 4 -- Inspect Room by Room
For each room you can optionally record a video walkthrough (max 2 minutes or 50MB; mobile records at 480p, desktop at 720p). Then rate each inspection category's condition using the 5-point scale: Good, Fair, Poor, Urgent Repair, or N/A. Add notes (mandatory if condition is not Good or N/A). Take multiple photos per category (compressed to 1024px JPEG). All data auto-saves every 1.5 seconds. Photo uploads happen immediately. If an upload fails, it queues locally and retries when back online.

Step 5 -- Review and Complete
The Report Summary page shows property details with GPS, room-by-room condition breakdown (colour-coded green/orange/red), photo thumbnails per category, and overall statistics (total items, condition distribution). Mark the report as "completed."

Step 6 -- Finalize
This is the locking step. After finalization, the report becomes immutable and cannot be edited. Every room must have at least one inspection item for validation to pass. A finalized_at timestamp is set. This is what makes the report court-ready.

Step 7 -- Generate and Share Your PDF
A professional A4 PDF is generated on the client side (no server required). The PDF includes company branding, report title, date, status badge, property details card, inspector name and agency, condition overview with statistics and progress bar, room-by-room detail pages with photos, notes, and condition badges, a legal disclaimer, and page numbers. The PDF is uploaded to cloud storage and a download link is saved. The report can also be shared via a public link that requires no login to view.

After generating the PDF, payment is required (unless covered by free tier or active subscription). The system checks for an active subscription, remaining free reports, or prior payment. If payment is needed, the user is redirected to Yoco. After payment, the report status updates to "paid" and the PDF becomes accessible.

5. CONDITION RATING SYSTEM
---------------------------

Every inspection item is rated on a 5-point scale:

GOOD (green, colour code #277020)
Meaning: No issues, acceptable condition. No comment required.

FAIR (amber, colour code #f5a409)
Meaning: Minor wear, cosmetic issues. A written comment is required explaining the issue.

POOR (red, colour code #c62121)
Meaning: Significant damage or deterioration. A written comment is required explaining the issue.

URGENT REPAIR (red, colour code #c62121)
Meaning: Immediate attention needed. A written comment is required explaining the issue.

N/A (grey, colour code #777777)
Meaning: Not applicable to this property. No comment required.

Rule: Any rating other than Good or N/A requires a mandatory written comment explaining the issue.

7. PAYMENT
-----------

Payment provider: Yoco (https://www.yoco.com) -- the dominant South African online payment processor.

Accepted payment methods: Credit card, debit card, Google Pay, Apple Pay (all via Yoco).

Payment flow:
1. User completes and finalizes a report.
2. The app checks if the user has an active subscription, free reports remaining, or has already paid for this report.
3. If payment is needed, the app creates a Yoco checkout session via a Cloud Function.
4. The user is redirected to Yoco to complete payment.
5. A webhook confirms the payment.
6. The app updates the report status to "paid" and the PDF becomes accessible.

Payment information is processed securely by Yoco and is NOT stored on CYA Pro servers.

Payments are non-refundable once a report PDF has been generated.

REPORT STORAGE AND RETENTION
Paid reports are stored securely in the cloud for 5 years from the date they were finalized. Each report includes a shareable public link and a QR code on the PDF, so anyone can access the report online at any time without needing a login. After 5 years, reports are automatically deleted. Unpaid reports (drafts that were never finalized and paid for) are automatically removed after 30 days.

Once a PDF is generated, the report is permanently locked and cannot be edited. This is by design -- an immutable, timestamped report is what gives it legal credibility as court-ready evidence.

Users can download their PDF again at any time during the 5-year storage period.


8. FEATURES
------------

OFFLINE SUPPORT
The app works fully offline. Data is saved locally using IndexedDB and syncs automatically when the user is back online. This is critical for South Africa where connectivity can be unreliable.

GPS VERIFICATION
Device GPS coordinates are recorded during property creation to verify the inspection location. On mobile, device GPS is used. On desktop, network location is used. GPS data is included in reports and is not used for tracking purposes outside of inspections.

PHOTO EVIDENCE
Multiple photos can be taken per inspection item. Photos are automatically compressed to 1024px JPEG format. Photos upload immediately; if upload fails, they queue locally and retry when back online.

VIDEO WALKTHROUGHS
An optional video walkthrough can be recorded for each room. Maximum length is 2 minutes or 50MB. Mobile records at 480p resolution. Desktop records at 720p resolution.

PROFESSIONAL PDF REPORTS
A branded A4 PDF is generated on the client side (no server required). Includes company branding, property details, inspector name and agency, condition overview with statistics, room-by-room detail pages with photos and notes, condition badges, a legal disclaimer, and page numbers. The PDF is uploaded to cloud storage.

SHAREABLE PUBLIC LINKS
Every finalized report gets a shareable public link. Anyone can view the report without needing a CYA Pro account or login.

AUTO-SAVE
All data auto-saves every 1.5 seconds. There is no manual "save" button needed.

TEAM MANAGEMENT (Agents and Contractors only)
Invite team members by email (up to 3 included with Agent Pro). Team members share the owner's subscription and can create unlimited reports. If an invited email has not signed up yet, they auto-link to the team on signup.

CONTRACTOR JOBS
Property owners and agents can create maintenance and repair jobs directly from the app. They select a property, choose from 14 trade types, set the urgency level, and describe the work needed. A shareable invite link is generated which can be sent to a contractor via WhatsApp, email, or any messaging platform. The contractor clicks the link, signs up in 3 simple steps, and is automatically linked to the job. No app download is required -- everything works in the browser.

14 Trade Types: Electrician, Gas Installer, Plumber, Builder, Painter, Tiler, Roofer, Carpenter, Locksmith, Pest Control, Waterproofer, Glazier, General Maintenance, Other.

Each trade has specific job types. For example, Electrician jobs include: fix lights, install geyser, fault finding, rewire, DB board, issue electrical CoC.

4 Urgency Levels: Low, Medium, High, Critical.

Job Status Lifecycle: Not Started → In Progress → Completed. Additional statuses: Awaiting Parts, Cancelled.

Photo Documentation: Contractors must upload before photos (required) and after photos (required). During photos are optional. All photos are timestamped.

Certificate Requirements: 5 job types require compliance certificate uploads before a job can be marked as completed: Electrical CoC, Gas CoC, Plumbing/Water CoC, Electric Fence CoC, Beetle/Pest Certificate.

Contractor Profiles: Contractors can build professional profiles including business name, mobile, province, town/city, service radius, primary and secondary trades, professional registrations (ECA, ECB, SAQCC Gas, PIRB, ECSA, SACAP, NHBRC, FPASA), certificates they can issue, documents they can provide (quotations, warranties, completion certificates), emergency callout availability, and base callout fee.

REPORT STATUS LIFECYCLE
Reports progress through these stages:
- Draft: Active editing, rooms and items being added.
- Completed: All rooms inspected, can still edit.
- Finalized: Locked and immutable, timestamp set.
- Paid: Payment confirmed, PDF accessible.
