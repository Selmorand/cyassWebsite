You only pay when your PDF is generated

Inspect, document, and review for free. Payment is only required when you generate your final PDF report. Once generated, the report is locked and cannot be edited — this is what makes it court-ready evidence.

Which One Should I Use?

Both give you the full CYA Pro experience. Here's how to choose.

Android App

  • Best performance on Android phones
  • Native camera access for faster photos
  • Better memory handling on older devices
  • Install once, open anytime
  • Recommended if you do inspections regularly

Web App (PWA)

  • Works on any device — phone, tablet, or computer
  • No installation required
  • Works on iPhone and iPad (Safari)
  • Full offline support
  • Contractors sign up and manage jobs directly in the browser
  • Great for one-off inspections or desktop use
