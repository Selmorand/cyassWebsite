CYA Pro is a property condition inspection tool. Reports are user-generated — CYA Pro provides the platform but is not the inspector. The accuracy and completeness of inspection reports is the responsibility of the user creating the report.

Solo Reports

Reports generated without a professional inspector present will carry a "solo report" disclaimer on the PDF. This does not reduce the validity of the report but indicates that it was self-conducted.

Report Finalization

Once a report is finalized, it becomes immutable by design. This is intentional — finalized reports are timestamped and locked to ensure they can serve as reliable evidence. Finalized reports cannot be edited or deleted.
