Contractor Workflow

Get invited to jobs, document your work, build your professional profile

Contractors document repair and maintenance work with before/during/after photos. Get invited to jobs via shareable links — no prior registration needed. Build a professional profile with trade registrations and accreditations. You also get 2 free inspection reports.

1 Get Invited to a Job

A property owner or agent creates a job and sends you an invite link via WhatsApp, email, or SMS. No prior registration needed.

2 Click the Link & Sign Up

Click the invite link. A 3-step public flow: confirm your identity, set a password, and accept the job. Everything works in the browser — no app download needed.

3 Complete Your Professional Profile

Set up your profile with your business name, mobile, province, town/city, and service radius. Select your primary and secondary trades from 14 options.

4 Add Registrations & Accreditations

Add your professional registrations (ECA, ECB, SAQCC Gas, PIRB, ECSA, SACAP, NHBRC, FPASA). Specify which certificates you can issue and documents you can provide.

5 View Your Job Dashboard

See all your assigned jobs with statuses, urgency levels, and property details. Manage multiple jobs at once.

6 Take Before Photos

Before starting work, upload photos documenting the current state. Before photos are required for job completion.

7 Start the Work

Update the job status to “In Progress”. Optionally upload during photos as you work.

8 Take After Photos

When work is complete, upload after photos showing the finished result. After photos are required.

9 Upload Compliance Certificates

For 5 regulated job types (electrical COC, gas COC, plumbing COC, electric fence COC, beetle cert), upload the relevant certificate before marking the job as completed.

10 Complete the Job

Mark the job as completed. The property owner receives a full audit trail with all photos, certificates, and timestamps.

11 Run Inspections Too

Contractors also get 2 free inspection reports. Create property condition reports just like any other role.
